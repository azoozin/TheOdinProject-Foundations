Lasagna

Description

A lasagna dish is a classic Italian casserole made with layers of wide flat pasta, a savory meat sauce, and a creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese sauce. The pasta is typically boiled until al dente, then layered with the sauces and grated cheese, such as mozzarella or Parmesan.

The dish is then baked until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the flavors have melded together. Lasagna is a hearty and satisfying meal that can be made with a variety of fillings, including vegetables, seafood, or different types of meat.
